About us

New Creative Advertising Co., Ltd, a wholly-owned subsidiary of New Creative Group, was established in 1997. Being a full service multi-media advertising provider, we have built up strong networks with various media and own a team of experienced professionals that can help our clients to reach their marketing needs in a cost-effective way with maximum efficiency and return on investment (ROI). For details of another subsidiary of the Group, New Creative Promotion Company, please visit : http://www.newcreativepromotion.com

An effective marketing promotion involves reaching customers in every possible means. We, New Creative, will support you to achieve this. Our services include:

  • Media planning and placement
  • Design
  • Printing
  • Online & Mobile Advertising
  • Public Relations
  • Event Production

Our team of professionals will help you through the whole process of designing & implementing your promotion needs. You can start with small budget yet gain satisfactory results. We will make the most use of your advertising budget by adopting flexible media planning depending on your different needs.

Our goal is to bring new thinking to our clients that can solve the problems they encounter in their marketing promotions. Human thinking is based on information, analysis and judgment. Unlimited creativity is an additional yet essential step in our team’s thinking process. We believe new thinking generates new possibilities.

We always equip ourselves with the most updated market trend and technology skills. We enjoy very much in working in this ever-changing industry and see continuous improvements as part of our lives. We hope our profession, passion, and dedication will bring our clients complete satisfaction.
